# City Profile - Agra

## Overview
Agra is a historic city in Uttar Pradesh, India, famous for the iconic Taj Mahal. Known as the "City of Love," Agra was the capital of the Mughal Empire and is home to three UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

## Key Areas
- **Taj Ganj** - Tourist area near Taj Mahal, hotels and restaurants
- **Sadar Bazaar** - Main commercial area, shopping and street food
- **Agra Cantt** - Railway station area, transport hub
- **Sikandra** - Akbar's tomb area, residential
- **Dayal Bagh** - Radha Soami temple area

## Transportation
- **Auto Rickshaw** - Most common local transport
- **E-Rickshaw** - Battery operated, eco-friendly
- **City Buses** - UPSRTC buses, very economical
- **Railway** - Well connected to major cities
- **Airport** - Agra Airport (limited flights)

## General Information
- **Population**: ~1.7 million
- **Climate**: Semi-arid, hot summers, pleasant winters
- **Time zone**: IST (UTC +5:30)
- **Languages**: Hindi, Urdu, English
- **Best time to visit**: October to March
- **Famous for**: Taj Mahal, Mughal architecture, Petha sweets

## Local Culture
- **Morning culture** - Early breakfast (Bedai, Jalebi)
- **Evening markets** - Sadar Bazaar comes alive 5-9 PM
- **Hospitality** - "Atithi Devo Bhava" (Guest is God)
- **Shopping** - Leather goods, handicrafts, marble items
- **Food habits** - Street food popular, vegetarian options abundant

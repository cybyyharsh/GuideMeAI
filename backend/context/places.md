# Places & Attractions - Agra

## Must-See Attractions
- **Taj Mahal** - World famous monument, best at sunrise/sunset
- **Agra Fort** - Red sandstone fort, UNESCO World Heritage site
- **Fatehpur Sikri** - Ghost city, 40km from Agra
- **Itimad-ud-Daulah** - Baby Taj, beautiful marble work
- **Mehtab Bagh** - Garden with Taj view, sunset spot

## Local Markets & Shopping
- **Sadar Bazaar** - Main market, leather goods, handicrafts
- **Kinari Bazaar** - Traditional items, wedding shopping
- **Shah Market** - Local shopping, everyday items
- **Taj Ganj** - Tourist market near Taj Mahal

## Hidden Gems
- **Chini Ka Rauza** - Persian-style tomb, less crowded
- **Ram Bagh** - Oldest Mughal garden
- **Guru Ka Tal** - Sikh gurudwara, peaceful place
- **Jama Masjid** - Beautiful mosque, local architecture

## Day Trip Options
- **Fatehpur Sikri** - Half day trip (40km)
- **Mathura-Vrindavan** - Krishna temples (60km)
- **Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary** - Nature lovers (55km)

## Local Areas
- **Taj Ganj** - Tourist area, hotels and restaurants
- **Sadar** - Main commercial area
- **Dayal Bagh** - Radha Soami temple area
- **Sikandra** - Akbar's tomb area

## Best Times to Visit
- **October to March** - Pleasant weather
- **Early morning** - Less crowds at monuments
- **Evening** - Good for markets and street food
- **Avoid** - Peak summer (April-June)

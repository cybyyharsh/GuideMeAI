# Traffic & Transportation - Agra

## Local Transportation
- **Auto Rickshaw** - Most common, negotiate fare
- **Cycle Rickshaw** - For short distances, eco-friendly
- **E-Rickshaw** - Battery operated, cheaper than auto
- **City Bus** - UPSRTC buses, very cheap but crowded
- **Tempo** - Shared auto for longer routes

## Popular Routes
- **Railway Station to Taj Mahal** - 6km, 20-30 minutes
- **Agra Cantt to Taj Ganj** - Main tourist route
- **Sadar to Sikandra** - 10km, Akbar's tomb route
- **Agra to Fatehpur Sikri** - 40km, 1 hour by bus/taxi

## Traffic Patterns
- **Rush Hours** - 8-10 AM, 6-8 PM (avoid main roads)
- **Market Hours** - Evening 5-9 PM (Sadar Bazaar area crowded)
- **Tourist Season** - October-March (more traffic near monuments)

## Parking Information
- **Taj Mahal** - Parking 500m away, shuttle available
- **Agra Fort** - Limited parking, early morning best
- **Sadar Bazaar** - Street parking, paid parking available
- **Railway Station** - Paid parking available
